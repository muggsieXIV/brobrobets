Meet our analysts

Hunter

Co-Founder


Hunter is a stock trader, sports gambler, and rapper. He is known for his strategic money risk management balanced with his hit or miss homerun parlays that can make the minor losses nothing compared to his big wins. Don’t get it twisted just because they call him the “Parlay Hunter” that does not mean he does not know how to protect those loses, usually his parlays wager minimal risk for high reward. Tap into his socials to get updated on his stock trading and investing tips.


Hunter is also a recognized artist in the rap industry with his hit record “Real Spit” featuring the prolific battle rapper DNA. Stay tuned and make some money with your new amigo Parlay Hunter.


Dre

Co-Founder


Dre Green, Entrepreneur, Northside Minneapolis Native, Co-Founder of BroBro Corporation, Brand Ambassador of NEX Outfitters Clothing Line, COM (Chief Marketing Officer) of Epimonia Clothing Line. Dre Green is an inspired and motivated entrepreneur with endless ambitious and creative ideas from Media Production content, Sports Entertainment Business ideas for the future of BroBro™️.


Dre Attended SMSU (Southwest Minnesota State University) where he earned a Bachelor of Science in Broadcast Journalism & Digital Media. He also spent time interning @89.9 KMOJ Radio Station (Only Black station In MN) where he shadowed Radio Personalities and develop tangible & intangible skills that he has carried with him in Co-founding BroBro Entertainment Group. Dre Believes in the next 10 years BroBro™️ will be a household name such as the bleacher reports & overtime & barstools of the world through its persistent & tireless work ethic from the founders of the company.


Dre was born June 17, 1993, he dedicates a lot to his Faith, Family & beloved step-father who passed away in 2016 the late great Bobby “MAIN” Williams.
